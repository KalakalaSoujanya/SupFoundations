Even though we are called by different names like NGO, charitable trust, Foundation, Committee etc., we are none of these. In short we call ourselves "Packers and Movers".Our agenda is to mobilize the happiness. That's why we say-"We buy and sell happiness ".
Let us take a moment to explain our tag line - We are selling happiness to our donors by giving them a platform wherein they can donate through the right channel (orphanages, old age homes, pet care, leper houses etc.)With all due respect to our donors, friends, members and contributors, we don't think that they are helping us or donating to us, rather we think that they are buying happiness from us.
